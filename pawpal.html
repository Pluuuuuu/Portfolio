Our Story

Introducing PawPal, the ultimate solution to modern pet care challenges. This AI-powered interactive companion not only ensures your pet is fed, hydrated, and entertained but also monitors their health around the clock. PawPal goes beyond basic care by offering customizable meal plans, interactive voice commands, seamless integration with smart home systems, and video calls between pets and owners. It can even dispense medication or supplements as prescribed. In addition to basic feeding and hydrating programs, PawPal monitors health and activity and engages pets with games. With two-way communication and BOT sessions, PawPal keeps your pet entertained while you are away, making it the perfect pet caregiver. Driven by our profound love for animals, we created PawPal to tackle the challenges of pet care in busy lives. Our journey is deeply personal, especially for our teammate Sarah, who manages ten pets while juggling her studies. We understand the difficulties of balancing pet care with daily responsibilities, which can lead to neglect or the tough decision to rehome beloved animals. With PawPal, we aim to ensure pets receive consistent attention and care through features like automated feeding, health monitoring, and interactive entertainment, so they never feel lonely or unwanted.
